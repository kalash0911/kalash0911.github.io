Payment Options

If you are concerned about your ability to get the dental care you need, we can help. We can coordinate your treatment plan with your dental insurance provider to determine what coverage you have. We will also explain other dental payment options available to you and help you with the application process.

Some of our patients from the Miami area choose CareCredit to cover the dental financing they need. This is a practical financing option for patients who have decided to finally achieve the smile they have been wishing for.

Of course, we also accept cash, checks and major credit cards. Please contact us if you need to know more about your dental payment options, CareCredit or other methods of patient financing for care.
