The Many Causes
of Bad Breath

To work out how to get rid of bad breath permanently, a dentist must start by determining which of the many causes is at work. They may take into account the following possibilities:

  • Dry mouth
  • Tooth, gum or mouth infections
  • Gum disease
  • Sinus infections
  • Poor dental hygiene
  • Smoking
  • Eating strong-smelling foods like garlic
  • Reflux disease
  • Medications
  • Poorly-cleaned dental appliances
  • Pneumonia
  • Tonsil stones
  • Liver problems
  • Parasites
  • Certain diets

As you can see, bad breath from the stomach, sinuses and other parts of the body may be the cause. The problem may not originate in the mouth.
