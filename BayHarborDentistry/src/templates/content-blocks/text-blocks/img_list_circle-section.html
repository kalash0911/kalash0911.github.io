Protect Your Health

The regular teeth cleanings we offer our patients do so much more than just keep teeth clean and sparkly. They have everything to do with maintaining their overall health.
A regular schedule of dental exams and teeth cleanings helps provide the following protection:

  • Reduction of tooth decay
  • Protection against serious gum disease
  • Prevention of tooth loss
  • Early diagnosis of mouth cancers
  • Reduction in risk of diabetes, heart disease, arthritis, and dementia related to gum disease
  • Early diagnosis of bruxism (teeth grinding) to prevent worn-down teeth

For all these reasons, we encourage our patients to maintain a regular schedule of dental exams and teeth cleanings. On Bay Harbor Islands, FL, we are your best choice for thorough preventative care that can contribute to a healthy and beautiful smile.
