Serving You Dentally, So You Can Rest Mentally

One of the most important steps for maintaining healthy teeth and oral structures is to visit us twice every year for a complete dental checkup. These routine visits allow our experts to keep track of your oral health and to administer timely treatment in the event that any issues are discovered. Our dental checkups include a professional teeth cleaning, digital X-rays when necessary and an oral cancer screening. Patients are expected to see a dentist every six months for a checkup to stay healthy.

Each time we see a patient in our Bay Harbor Islands office, we look for problems like tooth decay or gum disease. Our novelties

Intraoral Cameras

At Family & Cosmetic Dentistry Wellness Spa in Bay Harbor Islands, FL, we keep up with the latest dental technology so your treatment is always state-of-the-art. For that reason, we have equipped our office with intraoral cameras to ensure thorough treatment of any issue with your teeth and gums.

This handheld camera fits inside your mouth to record sharp images of every tooth as well as the shape of your gums and mouth. As an extra benefit, we can also show you any problem clearly during your dental exam. You can vividly see the inside of your own mouth and every tooth!

An intraoral camera can also be used in place of the gummy impression trays we used to use when planning bridges, crowns or Invisalign treatment. That’s a benefit our patients love!

Polishing, Too

When you get your teeth cleaned at your dentist’s office, it’s likely that your hygienist uses hand tools and a sonic cleaner on all the surfaces of your teeth and then follows this with polishing. The AIRFLOW system does two steps at once, incorporating both sonic cleaning and polishing. Completing two types of cleaning in one step saves you time. You may also find the AIRFLOW cleaning system more comfortable than earlier types of dental cleaning you’ve had. A dental hygienist typically follows AIRFLOW cleaning with manual cleaning of any tough, hardened spots of calculus.
