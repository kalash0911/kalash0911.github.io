Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Ryan AI collects, uses, and discloses personal information when you use our mobile application, Ryan AI. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect
We collect the following types of information:

1.1 User-Provided Information
When you use the Ryan AI app, we may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us. This may include:

  • Your name
  • Email address

This information may be used for subscription payments and to communicate with you.

1.2 User Generated Content: The information provided by users for text-based story generation is not shared with third parties and is used exclusively within the app for the purpose of providing personalized storytelling experiences to children.

1.3 Subscription Information
To facilitate subscription payments, we may collect and store relevant payment information. However, we do not store your full payment details. We rely on trusted payment processors to handle payment transactions securely.

Use of Information
We use the collected information for the following purposes:

3.1 Providing Services
We use your data to provide you with the services offered by Ryan AI, including generating and delivering text-based stories with AI-generated narration.

3.2 Communication
We may use your email address to send you important notifications, updates, and promotional materials related to Ryan AI. You can opt-out of promotional emails at any time.

3.3 Improving Services
We analyze usage patterns to enhance our application's performance, content, and features, providing a better user experience.

Data Retention
We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. We will securely delete or anonymize your personal information once it is no longer needed for its intended purposes.

Information Sharing
We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes or any other purposes beyond what is necessary to provide our services, including payment processing.

Data Security
We are committed to protecting your personal information. We employ industry-standard security measures to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Children's Privacy
Ryan AI is specifically designed for children aged 4 and above and is intended to be used under the supervision and guidance of adults or parents. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Parents or guardians are encouraged to monitor and supervise their child's use of the app and contact us with any concerns about data collection or their child's privacy.

Contact Us
If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the use of your personal information, please contact us at admin@ryan-app.com.
By using the Ryan AI application, you consent to the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy.
