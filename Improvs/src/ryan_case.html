about the project

Ryan is an app that wants to cover the niche of AI generated stories for children. Focusing fairy tales, bedtime stories and fantasy for its demographic helps us optimize for the ideal experience. We clearly portray that experience and its features.

Industry: fairy tales

Platforms:Ios, Android

Tech Stack: Project management, Back-end development, Ux/ui Design, Front-end development, motion design, QA, 3d designer

How it works
How it works

  • 1 Step

    Tell us about your children

    Ryan will use the initial information to write a children’s story for that age group. Further it uses the profile to personalize the stories to the kid’s likes.

    Get the App

  • 2 Step

    Create a Unique Fairy Tale

    Here Ryan provides all the tools needed to generate a story for kids that both they and the parent would love. Include lessons, characters, toys, animals and anything else that one could imagine.

  • 3 Step

    Immerse in Your Enchanting Story

    Watch as your children’s story comes to life. Each fairy tale can get to add further prompts or just let Ryan continue as it was.

  • 4 Step

    Give The Fairy Tale A Voice

    Ryan gives you the option to have a narrator read the tale. This is great for when the kid is laying in bed, playing, drawing ordoing just about anything else.

  • 5 Step

    Library of Saved Fairy Tales

    Ryan also hosts a library of saved children’s stories from all users willing to share. So even if you can’t think of something you can always discover and continue a loved story.
